SCOTCH EGG

List of the ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sausage beef
  • Black pepper
  • Salt
  • Seasoning
  • Egg {Boiled}

    STEPS:

    1. In a bowl,put the sausage beef.
    2. Pour in the pepper,seasoning,salt and flour then mix together.
    3. Flatten the beef and wrap in the egg.
    4. In a hot oil,fry till it turns golden brown and then enjoy your meal.
    DOUGHNUT

    List of ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Water

    STEPS:

    1. Mix all dry ingredients in a bowl and mix it all well.
    2. Pour in the water and mix till you get a stretchy and thick mixture.
    3. Leave to rest for about 10 mins.
    4. On a floured table,knead and flatten them cut out a round shape.
    5. Leave to proof and fry.
    CRIPSY POTATOES

    List of ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Bread crumbs

    STEPS:

    1. Slice potatoes to your desired shape.
    2. Pour in a salted water then rinse and let the water drain.
    3. Put in the flour,then egg and crumble.
    4. Fry in a hot oil.
    5. Then remove from the oil when done.
    VEGETABLES SALAD

    List of ingredients

  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Baked beans
  • Canned beef
  • Salad cream

    STEPS:

    1. Shread cabbage and carrots in a bowl.
    2. Pour in the baked beans and canned beef then mix.
    3. Mix all together with the salad cream and then refrigerate.
    4. Then serve.
